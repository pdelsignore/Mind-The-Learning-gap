Title: The D.E.E.P. Framework

Content:
A roadmap to unlock AI productivity through learning:

- Diagnose - Define your task augmentation plan
- Embed - Instill learning in the flow of work
- Evaluate - Measure progress with skills assessment
- Prioritize - Position learning as strategic investment

=============================================
Title: Diagnose

Content:
Define your task augmentation plan

- Conduct task-based analysis
- Identify your expert enthusiasts
- Build your augmentation squads
- Identify and roll out use cases

SLIDE 6: EMBED
=============================================
Title: Embed

Content:
Instill learning in the flow of work

- Create a culture of continuous learning
- Embed learning in daily workflows
- Enable social learning
- Emphasize durable skills

SLIDE 7: EVALUATE
=============================================

Content:
Measure progress with robust assessment

- Invest in ambient assessment methods
- Use AI to personalize learning
- Test skills in authentic conditions

SLIDE 8: PRIORITIZE

Title: Prioritize

Position learning as strategic investment

- Prioritize skills over roles
- Build a measurable skills ecosystem
